Design Choices

Colour Theme
We decided to go with purple and black for our headers and buttons, since these are the colours of our brand. They are also contrasting so that makes the website easily readable. For the main body of the pages we chose white to keep it simple but again clear.
Index Page
We wanted our Index Page to welcome and guide the website visitors through the website. We put our brand images and values, as well as links to all the other pages supported visually by a carousel.
Contacts Page
We dedicated a page to our contacts so it's easy for customers to reach us.
